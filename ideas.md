# Project Ideas & Inspiration

Stuck on what to build? Here are some ideas to get your creative juices flowing. These are starting points — the best projects often remix or extend an idea in an unexpected direction. Make it your own!

IBM Granite is a versatile family of AI models. Your project can use language models for text generation, summarization, Q&A, or code generation. See [Learning Granite](learning-granite.md) for details on what's available.

---

## 💡 Idea Categories

### 1. Study & Learning Tools
Help students learn more effectively using AI.

- **AI Study Buddy:** A chatbot that quizzes students on course material, explains concepts, and adapts to their level of understanding.
- **Lecture Summarizer:** Upload lecture notes or a transcript; Granite summarizes the key points, generates flashcards, and creates a study guide.
- **Textbook Q&A:** A RAG-powered app that lets students ask questions about a textbook or set of PDFs and get sourced answers.
- **Exam Prep Assistant:** Generate practice problems and worked solutions for a given topic.

---

### 2. Code & Developer Tools
Use Granite Code models to help developers.

- **Code Explainer:** Paste any code snippet and get a plain-English explanation of what it does — great for reading unfamiliar codebases.
- **Code Review Bot:** Submit a PR or paste code; the bot reviews it for bugs, style issues, and security concerns.
- **Documentation Generator:** Automatically generate README files, docstrings, or API docs from source code.
- **Debugging Assistant:** Describe an error and your code; the assistant suggests fixes and explains the root cause.
- **Unit Test Generator:** Input a function; output a suite of unit tests.

---

### 3. Productivity & Communication
Help people communicate and organize more efficiently.

- **Email Drafting Assistant:** Describe what you want to say; the assistant drafts a polished email.
- **Meeting Notes Summarizer:** Paste meeting transcript; get a summary, action items, and decisions.
- **Slack / Discord Bot:** A Granite-powered chatbot integrated into a team communication platform.
- **Report Generator:** Input raw data or bullet points; output a formatted professional report.

---

### 4. Research & Information Tools
Make information easier to find and understand.

- **Research Assistant:** Ask a question about a topic; the app searches documents or a knowledge base and synthesizes an answer with citations (RAG).
- **Bias Detector:** Analyze a piece of text and identify potential biases or one-sided arguments.
- **News Summarizer:** Summarize and compare multiple news articles on the same topic.
- **Citation Helper:** Paste a paper; the assistant helps you understand it and find related work.

---

### 5. Health & Wellness
AI tools that support well-being (responsibly!).

- **Mental Health Journal Companion:** A supportive journaling app where Granite helps you reflect on your entries (with appropriate safety guardrails using Granite Guardian).
- **Fitness Plan Generator:** Input your goals and current fitness level; get a personalized weekly workout plan.
- **Nutrition Advisor:** Input what you ate today; get nutritional insights and healthy alternatives.

> ⚠️ Health-related apps require careful attention to safety, accuracy, and responsible AI use. Consider using [Granite Guardian](https://huggingface.co/ibm-granite/granite-guardian-3.2-5b) to add safety guardrails.

---

### 6. Creative Applications
Explore the creative potential of language models.

- **Story Collaborator:** A co-writing tool where the AI continues your story while maintaining character voice and plot consistency.
- **Songwriting Assistant:** Input a theme or mood; generate song lyrics in various styles.
- **World Builder:** Generate detailed lore, characters, and settings for a tabletop RPG or creative writing project.
- **Poetry Workshop:** Get feedback on your poems and see how Granite would reimagine them in different styles.

---

### 7. Local/Community Impact
Build something that helps your campus or local community.

- **Campus Event Bot:** Answer questions about UVM events, deadlines, and campus resources.
- **Course Advisor:** Help students navigate course selection and degree requirements using a knowledge base of course information.
- **Sustainability Tracker:** Help users track and reduce their carbon footprint with AI-generated personalized tips.

---

### 8. Data Analysis & Insights
Use Granite to make sense of data.

- **Natural Language to SQL:** Let users query a database in plain English; Granite translates to SQL.
- **Data Story Generator:** Input a CSV or chart; the assistant describes the trends and generates an executive summary.
- **Sentiment Analyzer:** Analyze customer reviews, social media posts, or survey responses at scale.

---

## 🚀 Tips for Picking a Great Project

1. **Pick something you care about** — you'll work harder and smarter on a topic that interests you.
2. **Scope it right** — you have one week. Choose an idea that has a working prototype achievable in 2–3 days, leaving time to polish.
3. **Focus on the Granite integration** — your project should meaningfully use IBM Granite, not just treat it as a toy add-on.
4. **Think about the user** — who will use this? What problem does it actually solve for them?
5. **Document as you go** — you'll thank yourself at presentation time.
